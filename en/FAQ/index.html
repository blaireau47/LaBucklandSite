Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the "La Bucklandsur gravelle"?

La Buckland is a cycling gravel ride/race. The course is 99% gravel with a few easy sections off route. The elevation of the ride makes it a great challenge for all level of cyclist. Also for a quick french lesson "La Buckland" is kinda pronouced like "Lah Buckland sour gra-vail"

What makes the La Buckland unique?

The challenge, the mixe of landscape, the elevation gain and the on course security are some aspect that make this gravel event unique.

Is this a mountain bike race?

La Buckland is a course design for gravel bikes, but mountain bikes are more than welcome. We do understand that N+1 rule is quit exensive to apply. We do have a few Gravel bikes for rental.

Where does the La Buckland take place?

La Buckland takes place mainly in the Bellechasse-Etchemin region. It is 1h15 south of Quebec city at 2023 de l'Église, Notre-Dame-Auxiliatrice de Buckland . The course goes through the village of Buckland, St-Luc and uses the Parc du Massiif du Sud.

Is the course marked?

Yes, the course is marked the day prior to the event. You will find the GPS map 1 month before the race. Last year course is availlable, the 2020 course will have 10km more with a few offroad sections but will be very similaire to the 2019 course.

Can we use a GPS?

The course will be marked, but the use of GPS is permitted and a great idea. We will have the final course availlable a month prior to the event.

Can we have external support?

Yes, exterior support is allowed. We ask that all help and support be given in uphill section and never block the road.

Can my external support follow me?

No, for security reason, we ask all support not to follow riders and try to stay of course as much as possible.

What is the best tyre choice?

We had great success with 38mm Tubeless like these : Vittoria Terrono dry.
