July 11th 2020 in Buckland, Bellechasse-Etchemins, Québec

"La Buckland" the gravel cycling event in Québec.

"It ain't a race, it's a challenge but the first to cross the line is the fastest"

A course that will blow you away, two options: "La Buckland" or the "le petit Buck" (the Small Buck). A challenge for any cycliste that prefers riding gravel. The elevation gain will maque this ride an avanture for every level of athletes. The scenery on the course will blow you away and as well your legs !!

General information

2020 edition will centralise all activities around the headquarter:

  • Parking
  • Race pickup and registration
  • Start and finish
  • BBQ and after race party
  • Race animation
